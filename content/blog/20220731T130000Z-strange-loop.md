I have a deep fascination with trance music and the special vibe it creates. I prefer the older stuff when the genres weren't as defined and a good set would take you for a real ride through space and time.

This mix has been a labour of love. Most of the tracks are from 1995 - 2002 with strong representation from Gus Till on Flying Rhino records and Simon Posford on Twisted records and almost every tune is a dear favourite of mine. It's 3hrs long and it won't let you go. Enjoy.
